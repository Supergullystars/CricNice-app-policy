Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Super Gully Stars – Cricket App (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information when you use the application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

The app allows users to enter cricket-related data such as match details, team names, player names, scores, and statistics. All such data is stored locally on the user’s device only.

How We Use Information

The data entered by the user is used solely for:

  • Displaying cricket scores and statistics
  • Managing matches within the app

The app is fully controlled by the user, and no data is transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

This app displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user-entered data to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

All cricket-related data is stored locally on the user’s device using mobile storage mechanisms. We do not maintain any backend servers for storing user data.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

User Control

Users have full control over the data entered into the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
